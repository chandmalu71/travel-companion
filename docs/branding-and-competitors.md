## Competitor Landscape

| App | Strengths | Gaps (where we can win) |
|-----|-----------|--------------------------|
| **TripIt** (SAP Concur) | Best-in-class email parsing, auto-itinerary creation, business traveler focus | No AI search, no expense splitting, no social sharing, no dietary/allergy preferences, limited collaborative planning |
| **Wanderlog** | Good collaborative planning, POI discovery, map view, free tier | No receipt scanning, no expense splitting, limited email parsing, no check-in integration |
| **Google Travel** | Auto-pulls from Gmail, free, integrated with Google Maps | No standalone app, no offline mode, no expense tracking, no group features, no AI personalization |
| **TripAdvisor (Trips)** | Massive POI database, reviews, restaurant discovery | Weak itinerary organization, no email parsing, no expense management, no collaboration |
| **Splitwise** | Best expense splitting and group balances | Travel-only focus missing, no bookings, no itinerary, no POI/search |
| **Kayak Trips** | Flight tracking, price alerts, booking aggregation | No collaboration, no expense tracking, no AI search, no social sharing |
| **Sygic Travel** | Offline maps, day planning, POI discovery | No email integration, no expenses, no group features, limited personalization |
| **Lambus** | Group trip planning, expense splitting, shared lists | Limited AI, no receipt scanning, no check-in, basic email parsing |
| **TravelSpend** | Good expense tracking with multi-currency | No bookings, no itinerary, no collaboration, no AI features |

## Our Key Differentiators

The features that set this app apart from all existing competitors:

1. **AI-powered everything** — natural language search with dietary/allergy-aware personalization (no competitor does this well)
2. **Receipt scanning + group expense splitting** in one app (competitors split this across Splitwise + TripIt)
3. **Itinerary gap detection** — proactively tells you what's missing (unique feature)
4. **Flight check-in integration** — one-tap check-in from within the app
5. **Dietary/allergy preferences** influencing all recommendations (unserved market)
6. **Social media sharing with curated highlights** — post-trip storytelling built in
7. **Full offline mode** with sync — critical for international travelers

## Competitive Positioning

The closest all-in-one competitor is **Lambus**, but we beat it with AI personalization, receipt scanning, gap detection, and check-in integration. No single competitor covers our full feature set.
